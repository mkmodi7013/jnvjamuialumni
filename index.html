JNV Jamui Alumni Association

Online Alumni Network of JNV Jamui

Welcome to the official website of JNV Jamui Alumni Association.

Our alumni association, affiliated with Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Jamui, is dedicated to enhancing the alumni experience and fostering meaningful connections within the JNV community. Our primary goal is to create a robust network that supports the growth and development of both current students and alumni, ultimately contributing to the overall progress of our beloved alma mater. By providing essential resources, guidance, motivation, and financial assistance in various areas of interest, we aim to empower our members on their journeys to success. Join our vibrant community on social networks to connect with fellow alumni, access a plethora of benefits, and tap into the power of your alumni membership.

About us

Our members comprise alumni from Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Jamui (Bihar), and we stay closely connected through various social networking platforms, especially Facebook. The association is led by dedicated seniors and active members who ensure its smooth functioning both online and through school-level activities, including our Annual Alumni Meet.

What we do?

  • Career Counselling and Academic Support: We provide valuable career guidance and academic advice to current students and fellow alumni of JNV Jamui.
  • Professional Networking: Our platform aims to connect all alumni for the advancement of their professional careers.
  • Educational Exchange: We foster the exchange of educational guidelines and information among our alumni and students.
  • Financial Assistance: We offer possible financial aid to alumni or students pursuing their academic aspirations.

Join us in making a difference and strengthening the bonds that tie us together as proud members of the JNV Jamui family. Together, we can achieve greatness and support the growth of our alma mater.
